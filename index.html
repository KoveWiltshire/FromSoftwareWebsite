What is Fromsoftware?

FromSoftware is a video game company established Tokyo on November 1, 1986.

They started making business applications before they started making video games.

FromSoftware is most known for making the "Soulsborne" games the "Souls-like" genre.

Soulsborne games include games such as Elden Ring, Bloodborne, and the Darks Souls trilogy.



History of FromSoftware

FromSoftware's first game was King's Field. Released in 1994 for the PlayStation, it

was a 3D RPG and a pioneer of 3D games. The first Soulsborne game was Demon's Souls

released in 2009 for the Playstation 3. Demon's Souls success lead to the birth of the

Soulsborne games and the Souls-like genre. FromSoftware's biggest success was Elden Ring

which sold 28.6 million copies and won game of the year in 2022, but Dark Souls was the

game that made FromSoftware the company it is today being very hard yet very fun and having

great but challenging mechanics.
